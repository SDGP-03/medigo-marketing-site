About MediGo Driver App

MW Reviewed by MediGo Product Team

MediGo Driver App helps ambulance drivers receive assignments fast, navigate to pickup locations, and share live trip status with hospitals. It is designed for daily field use with a simple interface and low-friction workflow.

Key Features

  • Live assignment alerts: Get immediate notifications for new dispatch requests.
  • Integrated navigation: Open destination routes quickly to reduce response delays.
  • Trip status updates: Share en route, arrived, and completed status in real time.
  • Driver-focused UI: Large actions and clear screens for use during active operations.
  • Release-based updates: New app versions are published on this page with history.

Trust and Safety

The app is built for operational reliability and clear communication between drivers and dispatch teams. Drivers can verify assignment details before confirming actions, helping reduce route confusion and handling errors.

Privacy Notes

MediGo uses only the permissions needed for dispatch communication, phone operation, and location-based trip updates. Access can be reviewed and managed in Android settings based on your organization policy.

How to Use on Android

  1. Download the latest APK from the release section above.
  2. Install the app and sign in with your approved driver account.
  3. Allow required permissions for calls, notifications, and location.
  4. Keep internet and GPS enabled during active duty shifts.
  5. Accept assignments and update trip status from within the app.

Support

For deployment support, account setup, or issue reporting, contact your MediGo administrator or dispatch operations team.
